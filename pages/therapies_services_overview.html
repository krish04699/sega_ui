Our Natural Therapies

Discover our comprehensive range of natural treatment modalities designed to promote healing, restore balance, and enhance your overall well-being through time-tested therapeutic approaches.

Complete Therapy Services

Explore our full range of natural healing therapies, each designed to address specific health concerns and promote overall wellness

Medical Nutrition Therapy

We create personalized diet plans based on your health condition, constitution, and lifestyle. MNT corrects nutritional deficiencies, improves organ function, and promotes healing from within.

Acupuncture & Acupressure

Based on Traditional Chinese Medicine, this therapy involves stimulating specific points on the body to regulate energy (Qi), reduce pain, improve circulation, and support organ health.

• Acupuncture uses fine needles.

• Acupressure uses finger pressure.

Shuddhi Kriya

A yogic detoxification technique to purify internal organs and systems. These include: • Neti (nasal cleanse) • Vamana (therapeutic emesis) • Basti (colon cleansing) • Trataka (eye purification)

Yoga Therapy

Therapeutic yoga is tailored to your specific health needs. It combines physical postures, breathing techniques, and relaxation to promote recovery, calm the mind, and harmonize hormones.

Manipulative Therapy

Manual techniques to correct musculoskeletal alignment. Useful for relieving joint stiffness, posture issues, and spinal problems. It improves range of motion and reduces chronic pain.

Electro & Exercise Therapy

Electrotherapy uses low-frequency electrical currents to stimulate muscles, reduce inflammation, and relieve pain. Exercise therapy includes guided therapeutic exercises to strengthen weak muscles and restore functionality..

Ozone Therapy

A modern naturopathic tool using medical-grade ozone (O₃) for healing. It improves oxygen delivery to tissues, boosts immunity, fights infections, and accelerates wound healing.

Mud Therapy

Cooling and detoxifying, mud is applied over the body or specific organs to draw out toxins and relax the nervous system. Beneficial in stress, inflammation, and digestive issues..

Cupping Therapy

A suction-based therapy that removes stagnation, improves blood flow, and clears toxins. Dry and wet cupping are used based on the condition.

Hydrotherapy

Water in various temperatures and pressures is used to stimulate circulation, reduce inflammation, and tone the body. Includes steam bath, hip bath, spinal bath, foot bath, and jet spray.

Plantain Leaf Bath

An ancient Siddha-based therapy where the body is wrapped with plantain leaves to absorb heat and toxins. It’s deeply cooling, anti-inflammatory, and helps in skin and stress-related disorders.

Foot Reflexology

The feet have reflex points that connect to internal organs. By stimulating these zones, reflexology promotes healing, balances energy, and supports organ functions.
