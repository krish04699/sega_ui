About SEGA Naturopathy , Yoga & Ozone Therapy Clinic

At SEGA , we believe in the body's innate ability to heal itself when given the right support. Our clinic is a sanctuary for those seeking holistic, drugless, and personalized care for chronic and lifestyle-related conditions.

Founded by Dr. S. Divyasarathy (BNYS, M.Sc (Clinical Nutrition & Dietetics), CCIM – NIMHANS), SEGA integrates the time-tested wisdom of Naturopathy and Yoga with the scientific innovation of Ozone Therapy. With a deep-rooted passion for natural healing and a patient-first approach, Dr. Divyasarathy has helped transform the lives of individuals battling obesity, diabetes, autoimmune diseases, pain disorders, and more.

Our Vision

Our Mission
